The Internet

The Power That Made Us Who We Are

In modern days, WiFi allows us to use any device to connect to the internet from anywhere wihtin the range.


Here's a brief history of the internet:

  • 1960's - MIT computer scientist J.C.R. Licklider comes up with the idea of global network. In 1969, group of researcher finally developed functioning prototype.
  • 1970's - Kahn and Cerf collaborated to develop network protocol, which is the first TCP/IP. It allowed connect with multiple network. Also contributed to development of Unix systems.
  • 1980's - Internet became inexpensive due to dial-up connection so the general pulic has opportunity to use them. It also helped develop LAN system and Domain Name system.
  • 1990's - HTML was introdued, giving birth to World Wide Web. Many macroscale company, e.g. google, began to pop-up around this time.
  • 2000's - WiFi (and other wireless communication) as well as mobile internet devices were introduced. YouTube also appeared during this time period. Web-based media began to gain its popularity.
  • 2010'sOnline education becomes mainstream, rise of smartphones allows us to access to the internet from anywhere. We are now in the middle of a rise of augmented reality as well as artificial intelligence.
*Internet history timeline provided by a courtesy of Jefferson University
